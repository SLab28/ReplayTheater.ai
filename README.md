# ReplayTheater.ai

## Problem Statement

With the growing rates of social isolation and emergence of new intelligent computer systems people are turning to digital agents looking for companionship and social fulfilment. One of the essential needs that only a connection with another person can provide is psychological restoration and healing that occurs when we feel heard and re-experience our life stories in a protected environment. Playback Theaters were once the places where people would meet to exchange memories. With many aspects of social interaction moving to the digital space ReplayTheatre.ai
offers a new perceptual way to relieve past experiences. It is a unique form of improvisational theatre in which users volunteer stories from their lives and see them played back on the spot by the committee of 5 AI agents and live video generation pipeline.
